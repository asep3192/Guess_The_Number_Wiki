Medium Easy GameMode

Description: Medium Easy Gamemode is similar to Medium Gamemode but it slightly increases the probaility of winning and getting the number correct in 9 trys instead of 8. The number between 0 and 300 stays the same.

Where can I find Medium Easy Gamemode edition of Guess The Number

You can find Guess The Number - Medium Easy Gamemode Edition on the creators Github or from this Link: Comming Soon! Medium Easy Gamemode

In Medium Easy Gamemode of GTN you have 9 Guesses and you have to guess a randomly generated number between 0-300. This was mode was created as it says in the description to give the player a increase in chances of getting the number correct before running out of guesses. But you also get one more guess than GTN Medium Gamemode (The Next gamemode up).
